About 2AMature

Get to know the people and the story behind the music.

Biography

Formed in 2019 in Central Florida, 2AMature is a four‑piece alternative punk/garage rock band. The lineup consists of Geomar Berube (lead vocals & guitar), Micah Elmer (lead guitar), Brian Alvarez (bass) and Yanmark Berube (drums & backing vocals). Their playful name nods to late‑night jam sessions that solidified the band and their unapologetically DIY spirit.

The group blends punk energy with garage‑rock grit and surf‑punk melodies, drawing inspiration from the music they grew up on as well as the vibrant Central Florida scene. In 2020 they released their debut EP Life Guarding Elephant, featuring the songs “Water Socks,” “Green Boots,” “Ouija Board,” “Birthday” and “Daylight Debt.” Since then they’ve continued to write and record music that embraces spontaneity and humour without sacrificing musicianship.

Known locally as “The Best Band in the Universe,” 2AMature’s shows are equal parts sweaty punk spectacle and friendly hangout. Whether performing at their home base Uncle Lou’s Entertainment Hall or at DIY venues across the region, they foster an inclusive atmosphere where crowds can dance, surf and sing along. In 2025 they released the two‑song single Can’t Be Friends, following the 2024 single “Chef Boyz” and 2022’s DCxPC Live Volume 1 split with Call in Dead. With each release the band tightens their surfy riffs and playful lyrics while staying true to their punk roots.
