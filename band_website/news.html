Latest News

Catch up on our recent releases and announcements.

Preorder our split live record with The Snorts

We’re excited to announce that DCxPC Live Volume 32 is a split 12" featuring 2AMature and Albany punk outfit The Snorts. The live record captures our set at Uncle Lou’s Entertainment Hall in Orlando, while The Snorts’ side was recorded at El Dorado in Troy, NY. Preorders opened on January 20, 2025 and include a chance to win a rare test‑pressing.

One Question Interview

In January 2025 drummer Yanmark Berube joined Scene Point Blank’s One Question Interview series. When asked about collecting physical media, he replied that buying physical records is the only way to truly own music; digital purchases merely grant access that can be revoked. He also joked that his vinyl collection will double as throwing weapons when the AI overlords take over.

New single “Can’t Be Friends” out now

Our latest release Can’t Be Friends dropped on June 18, 2025. The two‑song single showcases our signature surf‑punk sound with catchy riffs and tongue‑in‑cheek lyrics. Stream it now on your favourite platform or check out our discography page for more details.
